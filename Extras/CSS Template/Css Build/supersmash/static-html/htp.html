HOW TO PLAY
Introduction

Welcome to HERO INDIAN SUPER LEAGUE 2022

It’s time to showcase your skill and go for glory!

Follow these 4 easy steps to get started:

  • Select A Match:
    Select any of the upcoming matches from any of the current or upcoming football series
  • Create Your Team:
    Use your football knowledge and showcase your skills to create your team within a budget of 100 credits
  • Join a Contest:
    Join the contest to win sponsored prizes and ultimate bragging rights to show off your skill!
  • Follow the Match:
    Watch the real match and track your fantasy scorecard

Create your Team

Step 1: Select a Match

Select any match from the list of upcoming ISL matches and click on the 'Create Team' button

Create your Fantasy team by picking 11 players within a budget of 100 credits.

Step 2: Create your Team

Select players for your Fantasy team from all 4 of the categories mentioned below:

  • GK: Goal-keeper
  • DEF: Defender
  • MID: Mid-Fielder
  • FWD: Striker

Check player info by clicking on the player display image.

Sort players as per their teams, credits or points.

Keep an eye on:

  • Number of players added to your team
  • Available credits
  • The deadline - Official match start time

Step 3: Select your fantasy team’s Captain & Vice Captain

After creating your fantasy team, choose a Captain & Vice Captain for the team.

  • Captain - Gets 2x points scored by him in the actual game
  • Vice Captain - Gets 1.5x points scored by him in the actual game

Managing your team

You can make unlimited changes to your team before the deadline.

You can also change your Captain & Vice Captain before the deadline.

Make sure you keep a tab on who is playing and who isn’t to keep your team updated at all times
