Rules
Team Creation
  • Select 11 players from different IPL teams within 100 credits.
  • You need to pick 1-4 wicketkeepers, 3-6 batsmen, 1-4 all-rounders and 3-6 bowlers
  • You need to pick a Captain (who will give you 2x points) and a Vice-Captain (who will give you 1.5x points)
  • You can pick a maximum of 7 players from a single IPL team
  • You can pick a maximum of 4 overseas players
Transfers
  • You have a total of 120 transfers (110 for the the group stage + 10 for the last 4 matches) over the course of the entire tournament
  • You can make unlimited transfers before the start of the 1st match and the 57th match (after the group stage)
